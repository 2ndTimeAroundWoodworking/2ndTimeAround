About Us

Rickie Lemons, founder and owner of Lemons Construction, brings new venture 2nd Time Around which creates products for the home. 2nd Time Around was founded on the idea of taking discarded old wood and giving it a second time to shine, and continues to carry reclaimed wood in many of its offerings.

His wife and co-owner, Benita Lemons, joins him.

Contact Rickie and Benita via Facebook message or phone for pricing, orders, and availability.

Join 2nd Time Around at festivals in North Carolina, Virginia, and beyond!
